Disaster Preparedness Toolkit

Being prepared can make all the difference. Assemble a "Go-Bag" with essential items.

  • Water & Food: A 3-day supply of drinking water and non-perishable food.
  • First-Aid Kit: Include bandages, antiseptic, pain relievers, and personal medications.
  • Flashlight & Radio: A hand-crank or battery-powered flashlight and radio.
  • Important Documents: Keep copies of IDs, insurance, and bank records in a waterproof bag.
